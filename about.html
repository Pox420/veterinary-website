Let Us Tell You Our Story

Allvet concern Pvt. Ltd. is a emerging and fastest growing importer and a marketing company of Nepal specialized exclusively in the marketing of animal health care products. Allvet is established in 2019 AD by experienced sales professionals, who have spent more than a decade in Nepalese veterinary industry, with the vision of providing quality veterinary medicine to Nepalese veterinary market. We cooperate with factories, which produce on GMP and GLP conditions, which guarantee a high standard and safety of medicine.
